Who We Are

Race 4 Water is a global non-profit organization committed to providing access to clean drinking water for communities in need and reducing ocean plastic pollution. We combine innovative solutions to tackle both environmental and humanitarian crises.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

Access to Clean Water

We work on sustainable water projects to provide clean water to remote communities around the world.

Protecting the Oceans

Our expeditions aim to raise awareness of ocean plastic pollution and promote sustainable solutions.

Our Key Initiatives

Water Purification Systems

Install water filtration units to provide clean, drinkable water in areas with no access to safe water sources.

Plastic Waste Collection

We organize expeditions and clean-ups to collect plastic waste from our oceans.

Education and Awareness

Raising awareness about the importance of clean water and reducing plastic usage globally.

Partnerships and Collaboration

Working with other organizations to amplify our impact.

Join Our Expeditions

Our expeditions around the world are a powerful way to raise awareness and make a difference. Each journey brings us closer to a cleaner ocean and communities with access to clean water.
